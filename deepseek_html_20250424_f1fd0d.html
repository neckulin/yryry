About The Competition

ARTMASTER is a global platform that brings together exceptional talent from around the world to showcase their creative vision. Since our inception in 2010, we've been committed to discovering and nurturing artistic excellence.

This year's competition features expanded categories including Digital Art, Mixed Media, Sculpture, and Experimental Forms. Our distinguished panel of judges will evaluate works based on originality, technical skill, and emotional impact.

Winners will receive cash prizes, international exhibition opportunities, and artist residencies at prestigious institutions. The grand prize winner will be featured in ArtForum and receive a solo exhibition in New York.
